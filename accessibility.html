accessibility

Mensura is for all of us. If something here isn’t accessible to you, tell us and we will do what we can within reason.

current posture

Every accommodation request is a signal. To include one more person is to return to proportion.

Our present failure: Care in the world too often arrives after exclusion. Mensura is still learning to begin with it.
